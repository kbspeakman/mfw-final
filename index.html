About me

Kabrina studied at Academy of Art University in San Francisco, where she received her Bachelors of Fine Arts in Advertising. During her time at Academy of Art University, she was a four year collegiate athlete that played volleyball.

Kabrina is a Graphic Designer/ marketing director at a small company in Fresno.
